Gerald Roofing Tiles — Sales & Installation

We sell, supply and install high-quality Gerald roofing tiles, known for their premium appearance, durability, and long lifespan. Nationwide supply available with payment on delivery. Ideal for modern residential and luxury buildings.

Aluminum Roofing Sheets — Sales & Installation

We sell, supply and install durable aluminum roofing sheets in Ibadan and nationwide, offering strong protection, affordability, and long-term performance. Payment on delivery available.

Roof Gutters Installation

Protect your building from water damage with professionally installed roof gutters. We ensure effective drainage for long-lasting structural protection across Ibadan.

Parapet Construction

We design and construct strong, well-finished parapets to enhance your roof structure, safety, and the overall architectural finish of your building in Ibadan.

Water Collectors Installation

Aluminum Windows Installation

We supply and install durable, stylish aluminum windows in Ibadan for homes and offices — combining security, modern aesthetics, and long-lasting longevity.

Wooden Carcass Construction

We construct solid, precision-cut wooden carcasses — the structural skeleton of your roof — ensuring a strong, properly aligned framework before your roofing sheets are installed.
